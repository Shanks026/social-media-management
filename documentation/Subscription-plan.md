# Tertiary Subscription Plans

This document provides an overview of the available subscription plans and their benefits for the Tertiary application.

## Available Plans

### 1. Ignite

- **Best For**: Freelancers & Solopreneurs
- **Price**: ₹1,999 / month
- **Features**:
  - **Client Portals**: Up to 5
  - **Internal Workspace**: Included
  - **Storage Limit**: 20 GB
  - **Whitelabeling**: None (No whitelabeling)
  - **Finance Module**: Basic ledger only
  - **Dedicated Support**: Email support
  - **Cost Per Extra Client**: ₹400 / month

### 2. Velocity (Recommended / Popular)

- **Best For**: Boutique Agencies
- **Price**: ₹5,999 / month
- **Features**:
  - **Client Portals**: Up to 15
  - **Internal Workspace**: Included
  - **Storage Limit**: 100 GB
  - **Whitelabeling**: Basic agency logo
  - **Finance Module**: Advanced invoicing & reports
  - **Dedicated Support**: Priority chat support
  - **Cost Per Extra Client**: ₹400 / month

### 3. Quantum

- **Best For**: Scaling Firms & Enterprises
- **Price**: ₹12,999 / month
- **Features**:
  - **Client Portals**: Up to 35
  - **Internal Workspace**: Included
  - **Storage Limit**: 500 GB
  - **Whitelabeling**: Full custom domain & email
  - **Finance Module**: Full CFO & profit analysis
  - **Dedicated Support**: VIP Concierge setup
  - **Cost Per Extra Client**: ₹370 / month
